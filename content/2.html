Senior Directory of Engineering in blockchain and crypto


Hi there! My name is Dan and I am a senior director of engineering at Circle, a leading global fintech company specializing in blockchain and cryptocurrency. Specifically, I am responsible for leading the infrastructure team for our stablecoin product, USDC.

For those unfamiliar, a stablecoin is a type of cryptocurrency that aims to maintain a stable value against a traditional asset, such as the US dollar. This stability makes it a popular choice for use in transactions and investments within the crypto world.

As a senior director of engineering, my role is to oversee the technical development of our stablecoin product and ensure smooth and efficient operations across our infrastructure. This involves managing a team of skilled engineers, setting project priorities and timelines, and making strategic decisions to drive the success of our product.

With my extensive experience in both blockchain technology and management, I am able to bridge the gap between technical solutions and business objectives. This is especially crucial in the rapidly evolving world of cryptocurrency, where innovation and scalability are key.

At Circle, we are committed to building a more open, inclusive, and equitable global financial system through the use of blockchain and digital currencies. My role in this mission is to ensure that our infrastructure is robust and efficient enough to support and drive the growth of our stablecoin product.

I am constantly learning and adapting in this dynamic industry, and I am excited to be at the forefront of the blockchain and crypto revolution.
